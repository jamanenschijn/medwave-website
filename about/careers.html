Careers

Join a small, experienced team where your work directly shapes clinical trials and patient outcomes.

Working at Medwave

Medwave is a flat, no-nonsense organisation. We don't have layers of management between you and the work. If you join us, you will work directly with experienced directors on real studies — not on support tasks for someone else's project.

We work across oncology, cardiology, and medical imaging, which means the work is varied and technically demanding. We value people who take initiative, know their field, and can operate independently when needed.

What We Value

Hands-on Expertise

We hire people who have done the work, not just managed it from a distance.

Pragmatism

We solve problems efficiently and avoid unnecessary complexity or overhead.

Ownership

Everyone at Medwave takes responsibility for their work and their results.

Scientific Rigour

The studies we run affect patients. We take compliance and data integrity seriously.

Collaboration

Small team, shared goals. Everyone contributes across disciplines when needed.

Flexibility

We work across the EU and support flexible arrangements for the right people.

Open Applications

No current vacancies — but we're always open

We don't always advertise positions formally. If you have a background in clinical research, medical imaging, biometry, or clinical trial technologies and you think you'd fit at Medwave, send us an open application. Tell us who you are, what you've done, and what you're looking for.
