Central Imaging in Cardiology

Central imaging collection and analysis for cardiology studies — ECGs, angiographs, IVUS, OCT, CT and more.

Cardiology Imaging Services

Medwave Medical Imaging provides central imaging collection and analysis for cardiology clinical studies. Our platform handles multiple image types used for endpoint analysis, with no mandatory software installation required at sites.

Image Types Supported

ECG

Electrocardiogram collection, quality control, and central analysis for cardiac safety and efficacy endpoints.

Angiography

Central angiograph collection and core lab assessment for interventional cardiology trials.

IVUS & OCT

Intravascular ultrasound and optical coherence tomography for detailed vessel wall assessments.

CT Imaging

CT-based imaging for structural heart assessments, calcium scoring, and coronary artery analysis.

Additional Services

No Software Installation

Sites access the imaging portal via the web. Any location with internet connectivity can participate.

Core Lab Workflows

Structured core lab assessment workflows for standardised, blinded endpoint analysis.

Medical Proctoring

Medical proctoring services to support investigators and ensure procedural consistency across sites.

Data Transfer

Secure imaging data transfer for DSMB and CEC meetings, ensuring timely access for safety reviews.
