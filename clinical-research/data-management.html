Data Management

Robust data collection, validation and management solutions for your clinical trial.

Data management built around neuro-oncology, not adapted for it. We bring protocol-specific eCRF templates, automated data checks and integrated oversight tools to trials where data complexity is the norm, not the exception.

Neuro-oncology eCRF Template

We developed a neuro-oncology eCRF template over years of trial experience. We adapt it to your protocol, so set-up is faster and the structure is already fit for purpose.

Integrated Data Oversight

Purpose-built automated scripts check incoming data continuously, flagging discrepancies and missing entries as they arise. Our in-house developed visualisation tool integrates all relevant patient data into a single interactive timeline, giving monitors a single contextual view of each patient, making it easier to assess clinical coherence and identify gaps.

Publication Data Support

We support the full journey from database lock to publication. Beyond cleaning and locking the database, we build analysis-ready datasets for specific endpoints and publication needs. Having managed the data from collection through to lock, we know exactly what is in it and can work efficiently with authors and statisticians to get papers across the line.

Paper QoL to eCRF

Paper-based QoL questionnaires are common in neuro-oncology. We have developed an in-house tool that reads scanned paper QoL forms and converts them into data ready for direct import into the eCRF, reducing site burden and minimising transcription errors.
